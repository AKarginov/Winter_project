about us

Welcome to Snow Forest Hikes! We are a community of outdoor enthusiasts who are passionate about exploring the beauty of nature in the winter. Our hikes take place in the snow-covered forests of the beautiful mountains, where we get to experience the unique and stunning views that only a winter hike can offer.

Our hikes are designed to be challenging, but also suitable for people of all skill levels. We offer a variety of different trails to choose from, each with its own set of unique features and challenges. Whether you're an experienced hiker looking for a new adventure, or a beginner looking to try something new, we have something for everyone.

One of the things that sets Snow Forest Hikes apart from other hiking groups is our commitment to the environment. We believe that it's important to respect and protect the natural world around us, and we take steps to minimize our impact on the environment. This includes using eco-friendly equipment and practicing Leave No Trace principles.

warm up and chill

After a long hike, the sauna is the perfect place to warm up and relax. Hiking in the mountains, the woods or the countryside can be a great way to get some exercise and fresh air, but it can also leave you feeling cold, tired and achy. The sauna offers a warm and comfortable environment where you can thaw out, unwind and ease your sore muscles.
